ABOUT US


We are a team of Virtual Assistants passionately helping busy Bussiness owners and entreprenuers by maintaining their websites, social media accounts and others so that they have more time to attract more ideal clients!

We are a group of VA's from different countries. We started the Virtual Community in 2019.

There were only three of us when we started building the virtual assistant community, We also went through trials, we also got to the point where we wanted to stop because we were not getting anything, until we got our first client who helped us get more clients, when we slowly got up we recruited employees to grow our community, then year go by and our community became stronger because of our unity.

Even though we were born in different places and we have different races we still have unity, not because we work together but because we help each other to make us better virtual assistants, and our friendship serves as a pillar for us to be more stable and compatible for each other.

For the past year we developed a lot of skills individually, we thank the clients who trust us to work with them they help us improved our skills and earn experience, in return for their trust we repay them by doing the job well and make an extra effort for the job. we performed the job very efficiently with speed and accuracy.
